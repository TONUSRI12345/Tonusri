The ‘cryptocurrency’ is the latest idea where the concept has stemmed from the necessity of the decentralized transaction, where no particular person or organization will monitor the transaction. The traditional money transfer needs one trusted third party to monitor the transaction. Where the discussed cash transferring system avoid that necessity, not only reducing the complexity over the currency transfer, but also making it a lot quicker process and cheaper than usual. 

The first ever cryptocurrency released in 2009, famously known as ‘Bitcoin’- the decentralized cash transfer system developed by a group or an individual, known as Satoshi Nakamoto. Other than Bitcoin, there are significant numbers of cryptocurrencies as well.
